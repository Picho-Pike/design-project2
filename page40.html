Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal



Which plan?

Going to the left, you feel like this is the right way. Sure enough, you round a bend and the manor is visible through a garden gate. You exit the maze of plants and find Mark waiting for you on the other side. "How did you. . ." You say but Mark cuts you off, "I created my own path, straight through the garden." Mark turns towards the manor house and you start climbing up, using the fancy windows as steps to boost your way up. You get to the second floor roof and decide to take a break at a window with a balcony.
