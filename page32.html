To steal or not to steal



Which plan?

Off to the right. After going straight then turning right, you find yourself at a fork with a path leading back towards the willlow tree. You ignore this path and go to the left. Hurrying down the path, you hear people rustling even further to the left. Now you find yourself at a left and right intersection. Not very difficult, you figure, if there are lots of people to the left, I won't go there. You head right and find yourself face-to-face with a gardener emerging from a left passageway.





If you have knives you can cut him down
