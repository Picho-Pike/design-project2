Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

Your daggers! You reach to your belt and draw your knife. They better leave me alone, you hope. The ruffians, unfazed advance on you. Two of them swing their weapons at you and you are forced to leap back to avoid the battering of their blows. You are pressured into the alleyway, but ever the opportunist, you wait for the perfect moment to strike. Sure enough, one of your attackers stumble a swing and you dive in. . . Only to be thrown to the wall by an unseen club. The force of the blow knocks the blade right out of your hand. Winded, you try to stand up only to have a hand at your throat. "Time to pay." The lead thug says as he slams you into the wall. Your arms flail wildly as you try to pry his hand away from your throat ineffectively. In desperation, you grab your other dagger out of your boot and force it into his gut.
