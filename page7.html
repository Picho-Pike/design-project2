Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

The merchant isn't worth your trouble, you figure as you chase after Mark, a merchant in the poor district will be stolen from by someone anyways, there's no point in risking the mission for a few coins. You and Mark exit the poor districts and enter the rich section of the city. The Baron's Manor house is easily visible from its perch on the hill. There will be plenty of opportunity to steal when you get to the Baron's manor.
