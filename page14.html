Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

The lead ruffian's hand slackens as he starts coughing blood. "Why you. . ." the ruffian gurgles as he leans more and more on you. Suddenly, he collapses against you and falls to the ground, releasing your neck and allowing you to gasp air back into your lungs. "You killed him!" One of the shellshocked ruffians say. You try to say something, but your crushed voice box doesn't feel like saying anything at all. A ruffian advances on you and swings it's club into your abdomen, sending you flying to the side. you land in a heap, too injured to try to get up. The thugs circle you and start beating you with their clubs. Beaten and broken, you watch as a club descends for your half-closed eyes. You feel immense pain, then nothing as you enter a blank void. The End
