Weapons or stealth?

'"It's a stealth mission," you say, "we don't need weapons". You turn to the left passageway and descend a spiral staircase to the ground floor of the hideout. After a length of fifteen minutes, Mark joins you with a sword and 2 daggers around his belt. "Come on, we have a stealth mission to accomplish" Mark says sarcastically. Your insides boil at Mark's insult, but you don't let it show, instead you exit the hideout and walk down the one-way street, with your destination peeking out over the tops of the common buildings.
