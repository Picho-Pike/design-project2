Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal



Which plan?

Left you go, turning a sharp corner and running straight then you turn another sharp corner and run along that path. A little hidden garden peeks out from the left side. As you come up to this little garden, you are shocked by how many people are there. A dozen of gardeners fill this little nook and they all pour out and surround you. You try to force your way past them but they stop you and knock you out with their shears. The End.
