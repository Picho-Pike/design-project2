Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal



Which plan?

Going the right way, you round a corner and there it is, the exit. Somehow though, Mark is standing there, at the exit, covered in leaves and scratches. You sigh, somehow Mark always beats you. You start your climb up the fancy windows and make your way to a large window with a little balcony in front to rest up.
