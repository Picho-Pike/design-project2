Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

Those thugs don't have a right to your prize, you decide. Let them try and rob the merchant, they won't find anything on him! While the merchant is in mid argument over the meager quality of the stall's produce, you strike, pretending to trip, you stumble a step and lift the money right off of his belt. You discretely attach the pouch to your belt and walk after Mark.
