Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

As you're catching up with Mark, you feel a heavy hand on your shoulder. You spin around and feel the air blasted out of your chest as a club comes crashing into your ribs. You fall to the ground, winded as one of the street thugs from earlier leans over you and spits on your tunic. "You stole our prize thief, so we're gonna steal something of' yours. Ain't that right boys!" The three other thugs, all holding various bludgeons, raise their weapons and cheer. You start to stand up to defend yourself and get kicked back onto the ground. "Don't even try, thief." The lead thug grabs you by the shirt and throws you towards an alley. You land sharply on your wrist, little stones cutting into your palm like daggers.
