Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

"Come on fellows, we can work this out." you say as you back your way up the street. "Here, have the money." You throw the pouch on the ground and run down the street. "Leave him boys!" You hear the lead thug say, "he ain't worth the effort, we already got the money." You step into line with Mark, a little winded and scratched up, but altogether ready for your grand theft at the Baron's manor.
