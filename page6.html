Weapons or stealth



To steal or not to steal

You give in to the temptation and advance on the merchant. After all, a rich person such as this shouldn't be shopping in here, you tell yourself. As you walk towards him, you catch glimpse of a group of street thugs walking in your direction. They might be after the merchant too, and judging by their clubs on their backs, you don't believe that the encounter will be peaceful. Should you warn the merchant, go through with the steal, or should you ignore this entire event and go back to Mark.
